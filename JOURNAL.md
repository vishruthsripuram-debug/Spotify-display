# 3/12/2026 10 AM - Spotify player

_Time spent: 1h_

I designed In fusion 360 the main body to be 3d printed for the controller. I built the base added in the components and organised them to fit. I plan on adding screw holes.

I first created a rectangular body on the y plane to contsruct the main body and then extruded it outwards. After the extrusion i created a hole in the front for the display and a hole in the back.

Once i did that i added in the screen to see what it would look like. Then i moved ontot adding the base for the microcontroller.

I created a shell around the mcu and added a lip to stop vertical movement.

I moved onto making the back shall after that. The back shell is intended to be friction fitted and has a cutout for the usb c port on the board.
